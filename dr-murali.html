About Dr. Murali

Dr. BK Murali is a practicing physician at Hope Hospital, Nagpur, and one of central India's most active AI entrepreneurs. His journey bridges two worlds: the clinical precision of medicine and the transformative power of technology.

Over the past several years, Dr. Murali has built more than 35 AI-powered products under the HopePhoenix brand, targeting healthcare, industrial automation, and enterprise efficiency. His vision is simple: Indian institutions deserve world-class software, built by people who understand how they actually operate.

As a Director at Bettroi (Dubai Silicon Oasis), he connects India's talent and healthcare market with GCC's capital and enterprise demand. His company DrM Hope Softwares focuses on clinical informatics, EMR systems, and voice-first documentation tools for India's hospitals and clinics.

Dr. Murali is a proud member of BNI Diorite, Nagpur, where he actively exchanges referrals with professionals across sectors. He welcomes 121 meetings with anyone in healthcare, industrial manufacturing, education technology, or enterprise AI.
