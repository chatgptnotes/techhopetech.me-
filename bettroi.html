About Bettroi

Enterprise AI, Edge-to-Cloud

Bettroi is a technology company headquartered at Dubai Silicon Oasis, building intelligent software platforms for enterprises across energy, oil & gas, healthcare, and education. Every product we ship is designed to replace manual operations with autonomous, AI-driven workflows.

Our platform spans the full stack, from edge devices and PLCs to cloud dashboards and enterprise analytics, giving organisations a single operating system for their business.

Co-founded by Biji Thomas and Dr. BK Murali, both active BNI members, Bettroi combines deep domain expertise in industrial engineering and AI with a startup execution culture.
