AI Services Available in Nagpur

Hospital Management Systems (HMS)

IHMS (Integrated Hospital Management System) and HMS+ are designed for Indian hospitals, with built-in support for Ayushman Bharat, ESIC, CGHS, and ABDM (Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission) compliance. They cover OPD, IPD, pharmacy, laboratory, billing, and patient records in a single platform.

Clinical AI Tools

CliniVoice turns doctor dictation into structured EMR entries, reducing documentation time by up to 70%. Aide provides clinical coding support and AI-assisted discharge summary generation. These tools are built for India's busy outpatient environment.

Industrial SCADA and Automation

SCADA.work and FactoryPulse provide real-time monitoring of industrial equipment, process parameters, and energy consumption. These systems are deployed in manufacturing plants, power utilities, and process industries. Nagpur's manufacturing sector (automotive components, textiles, pharmaceutical) is an ideal fit.

Enterprise AI Agents

AgentX and BOS (Business Operations Suite) enable enterprises to deploy AI agents for customer service, internal knowledge management, process automation, and decision support. Suitable for any organization with repetitive knowledge-work workflows.

Education Technology

AI-Shu is a personalized AI tutoring platform designed for Indian students, with curriculum alignment to CBSE, ICSE, and Maharashtra state boards. Ideal for schools, coaching centers, and EdTech ventures in Nagpur.
