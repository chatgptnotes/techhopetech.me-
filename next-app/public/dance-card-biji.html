Bio & Background

Professional Bio

Biji Thomas is an entrepreneur and technology leader with 25 years of experience at GE and across global businesses. For the past 5 years, he has been running BETTROI in Dubai, blending deep technical expertise with a passion for business orchestration.

He leverages AI tools to drive productivity, profitability, and customer experience for clients across diverse industries. Under the Bettroi umbrella, Biji has built 25+ AI products spanning 5 verticals, including enterprise workflow, energy, healthcare, industrial IoT, and hospitality.

With a Six Sigma Green Belt and a record of building from zero, Biji brings both engineering rigor and founder-level hustle to every engagement. His mission is to make AI actionable for the businesses that need it most.

Burning desire: to positively impact a million lives.
