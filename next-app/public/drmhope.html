Our Mission

Healthcare intelligence,
built from the inside

DrM Hope Softwares was founded on a simple belief: the people who understand healthcare best are the people who deliver it. Our founder is a practicing physician in Nagpur who saw, first-hand, how broken the software layer was in Indian healthcare and decided to fix it.

We build tools that work in the real conditions of Indian clinics, government insurance systems, and community health networks, not just in polished demos. Every line of code is validated against what actually happens on a ward floor.

Beyond healthcare, DrM Hope Softwares is expanding into professional networking (Linkist) and social event technology (PrewedAI), applying the same principle: solve a real problem, do it with AI, and make it work for people in Tier-2 India.

Three Products, Three Problems Solved

Each product was built to address a specific gap in Indian healthcare, professional networking, or the event industry.

A

AdamRIT

Healthcare AI

AI-driven ESIC patient mapping and referral intelligence system. AdamRIT reads the complexity of India's Employees' State Insurance Corporation network and automatically maps beneficiaries to the nearest appropriate specialist hospital, cutting referral delays and preventing insurance benefit lapses.

Case Study

Mapped ESIC patients across Nagpur, linking government insurance beneficiaries to specialist hospitals. Reduced manual referral paperwork and improved benefit utilisation for workers in the Vidarbha industrial corridor.

Events AI

AI-powered pre-wedding planning platform. Uses machine learning to match vendors, venues, and themes to a couple's budget, style preferences, and guest count. Brings the efficiency of technology to an industry still largely run on phone calls and WhatsApp forwards.
