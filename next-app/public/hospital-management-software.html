India's Healthcare Digitization Opportunity

The National Health Authority's Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) is India's most ambitious healthcare digitization program. It mandates digital health records, interoperable systems, and unique Health IDs for every citizen. Hospitals not using ABDM-compliant software will be excluded from government scheme empanelment.

HopePhoenix products (IHMS, HMS+, CliniVoice) are built with ABDM compliance as a core requirement, not an afterthought. They support HL7 FHIR data exchange, ABHA health ID integration, and full electronic health record (EHR) interoperability from day one.

FAQ

Common Questions

What should I look for when selecting an HMS for an Indian hospital?

The five non-negotiables are: ABDM compliance (for ABHA health ID integration and government empanelment), support for Indian government schemes (ESIC, CGHS, Ayushman Bharat), local language support (Hindi, Marathi, regional), a vendor with on-site support capability, and a pricing model that doesn't require a large upfront investment. HopePhoenix products satisfy all five.

How long does HMS implementation take?

For clinics and small nursing homes (under 30 beds), implementation is typically 1 to 2 weeks including staff training. For medium hospitals (30 to 150 beds), 3 to 5 weeks. For large hospitals above 150 beds with complex workflows and data migration from a legacy system, 6 to 10 weeks. All timelines include parallel running with your existing system until staff are confident.

Can my existing patient data be migrated into IHMS?

Yes. HopePhoenix has a dedicated data migration toolkit that handles imports from common HMS formats (Excel exports, CSV, and major legacy HMS systems used in India). Patient demographics, billing history, and clinical records can be imported. The migration is done in a test environment first, verified, and then cut over to production with zero data loss.

What is the pricing structure for IHMS and HMS+?

Pricing is a monthly SaaS subscription based on bed count. There are no per-user fees, no per-transaction fees, and no upfront license costs. Implementation and initial training are included. Contact Dr. Murali at +91 9373111709 for a custom quote: the price depends on your bed count, the modules you need (IHMS vs HMS+), and whether you need add-ons like CliniVoice or Adamrit.

Does HopePhoenix support hospitals outside Nagpur?

Yes. While on-site visits are naturally easier for Nagpur and Vidarbha-region hospitals, HopePhoenix software is deployed across India. Remote implementation, training via video call, and cloud-based support are available nationwide. For hospitals in Maharashtra outside Nagpur, on-site visits can be arranged.

Is CliniVoice sold separately or only with IHMS?

CliniVoice works best as an add-on to IHMS or HMS+, since it maps dictated content directly into the HMS patient record fields. However, for hospitals that already have an HMS and just want voice documentation, CliniVoice can be integrated via API. Contact us to discuss integration with your existing system.
