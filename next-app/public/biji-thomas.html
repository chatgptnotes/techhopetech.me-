About Biji Thomas

Biji Thomas is a technology entrepreneur based at Dubai Silicon Oasis, one of the UAE's premier technology free zones. As CEO of Bettroi.com, he leads a team building enterprise-grade AI platforms for clients across the Gulf Cooperation Council and global markets.

His product portfolio spans more than 25 solutions, with deep concentration in energy management, oil and gas operations technology, industrial IoT, and enterprise workflow automation. Bettroi's flagship products (AgentX, AssetX, PermitX, Ampris) are deployed by organizations in the energy and industrial sectors across the GCC.

Biji is the co-founder of Betser Life, a health and wellness technology platform connecting patients, practitioners, and healthcare supply chains. He partners closely with Dr. BK Murali on cross-region initiatives, bridging Dubai's enterprise market access with India's engineering talent and healthcare expertise.

As an active BNI member in Dubai, Biji leverages the world's largest business referral network to connect technology buyers with proven solutions, and to open doors for India-origin products in the GCC market.
